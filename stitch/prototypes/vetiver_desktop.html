The Ritual & Provenance

From the Silt of Kannauj to the Alpine Heights.

In the perfume capital of India, Kannauj, the vetiver grass is more than a botanical; it is a seasonal rhythm. Each root is harvested by hand during the brief window between the monsoons when the earth is most yielding.

"The roots hold the memory of the rain, exhaling a scent that is both woody and oceanic."

Following the harvest, the roots travel to our laboratory in Zurich. Here, using proprietary Swiss refinement techniques, we isolate the cleanest molecules of the 'Khus' oil, removing the harsh smokiness often found in industrial distillates.
