Description

Hand-pressed and aged for sixty days, our Triple Milled Khus soap offers a grounding olfactory journey.

Khus, or Vetiver, is known as the "Oil of Tranquility." This soap cleanses without stripping, leaving the skin supple and infused with an earthy, smoky scent that lingers like a memory of rain on parched soil.

The Ritual

01

Work into a rich lather under lukewarm water to release the grounding aromatic molecules.

02

Massage over damp skin in slow, circular motions, breathing deeply to evoke the scent of the forest floor.

03

Rinse clean. Place on a dry surface to preserve the density of the triple-milled structure.

Provenance Chip

Sourced from Kannauj

In the perfume capital of India, vetiver roots are hand-harvested after the monsoon. Our soap honors the traditional "Deg-Bhapka" steam distillation process that has remained unchanged for four hundred years.
